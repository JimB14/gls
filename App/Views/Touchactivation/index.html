TOUCH ACTIVATION = ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY

If you've ever used a smart phone or ATM touch screen, then you already know what touch activation is. Superior to mechanical switches, ArmaLaser believes that one day all advanced firearms accessories will use ArmaLaser-style switching.

Touch Activation vs. Mechanical Switch Activation

Mechanical Switch Touch Activation
Accidental Activation Very likely Not likely
Comfort Awkward feel on grip Perfectly natural grip
Compatibility Conditional on shape of hands Works with a wider range of hands
Immediacy Must reach for or apply pressure Instantly on by normally gripping pistol

Even mechanical switches have problems with heavily padded gloves. Use our new metalic-threaded ultra-thin glove patches to insure activation reliability with all types of gloves.
